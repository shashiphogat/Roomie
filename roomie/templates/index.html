About Us

Welcome to our student-built roommate finder!
Created by students for students at Chandigarh
University, our platform simplifies the search for
the perfect roommate. Our mission is to foster
community and connection by helping you find
a compatible roommate who shares your interests
and values. Join us in making university life more
enjoyable and memorable.
